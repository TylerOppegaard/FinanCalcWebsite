Dilution Per Share

Net Income = (I)
Preferred Dividends = (D)
Weighted Average number of shares outstanding = (S)
impact of convertible securities = (C)
impact of options, warrants and other dilutive securities = (O)

Equation

\(Dilution\ per\ Share = \frac{I − D}{S + C − O}\)
